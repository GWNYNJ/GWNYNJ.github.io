Workforce

In 2015, we placed 7,500 individuals with disabilities and other barriers to employment in jobs. These individuals are gaining independence and building careers.

People with Disabilities

1 in 7 of our retail employees has a disability. Goodwill employs individuals in our 41 retail stores throughout the Greater New York region. At Goodwill we are empowering individuals who are seeking opportunities to gain independence and dignity through the power of work.

Youth and Community

Goodwill serves youth ages 5-24 in programs located in Brooklyn and Queens. Our programs are designed to excite youth about their potential as they explore career pathways. Services include academic support, STEAM, specialized high-school prep, internships, mentoring, college tours and summer camp.
